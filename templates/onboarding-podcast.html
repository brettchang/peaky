How to use this form: All six fields are required for podcast placements. The more detail you provide, the more authentic and on-brand the host read will be. When complete, reply to your Peak contact with this document. We'll draft the script and send it for your approval before recording.
Script Direction
1. Campaign Objective *
What outcome do you want from this campaign? What does success look like?
 
2. Key Message *
What is the single most important thing listeners should remember? If they forgot everything else, what's the one takeaway?
 
3. Talking Points *
List the key points, claims, proof points, differentiators, or features the host should mention. Bullet points are fine.
 
4. Call to Action *
What should listeners do after hearing this ad? Include any promo codes, URLs, or specific instructions.
 
5. Target Audience *
Who are you trying to reach? What do they care about? What's their relationship with your product/service?
 
6. Tone / Brand Guidelines *
Any voice, style, or compliance guidance for the host read. E.g. "Conversational but professional", "Avoid mentioning competitors", "Must include our legal disclaimer: ..."
