# The Peak Media Co. - Email Agent Knowledge Base

## About The Peak

The Peak is a Canadian media company that publishes daily business newsletters and podcasts. Our advertising clients sponsor placements within our publications.

### Publications
- **The Peak** - Flagship daily business newsletter
- **Peak Money** - Personal finance focused newsletter
- **Peak Daily Podcast** - Daily business news podcast

## Placement Types

### Newsletter Placements
- **Primary** - Main sponsored content block (150-200 words). Premium positioning, 1 per day per publication.
- **Secondary** - Supporting sponsored content block (80-120 words). 1 per day per publication.
- **Peak Picks** - Short product/service recommendation (40-60 words). Up to 4 per day per publication.
- **Beehiv** - Beehiiv-native ad placement (100-150 words).
- **Smart Links** - Brief link-based ad (30-50 words).
- **BLS** - Below-line sponsorship (80-120 words).

### Podcast Placements
- **:30 Pre-Roll** - 30-second host-read ad at start of podcast.
- **:30 Mid-Roll** - 30-second host-read ad mid-episode.
- **15 Minute Interview** - Long-form sponsored interview segment.

## Campaign Lifecycle

1. **Onboarding to be sent** - Campaign created, onboarding form not yet sent to client
2. **Waiting for onboarding** - Onboarding form sent, awaiting client completion
3. **Active** - Client has completed onboarding, placements being managed
4. **Placements Completed** - All placements have been published
5. **Wrapped** - Campaign fully complete and closed

## Placement Status Flow (Newsletter)

New Campaign -> Copywriting in Progress -> Peak Team Review Complete -> Sent for Approval -> Approved

## Scheduling Policies

- Newsletter placements are scheduled on **weekdays only** (Monday-Friday)
- **Primary** and **Secondary** placements: maximum 1 per publication per day
- **Peak Picks**: maximum 4 per publication per day
- Other types have no daily capacity limit
- Lead time: We generally need at least **5 business days** notice for scheduling
- Clients should provide copy/assets at least **5 business days** before the scheduled date

## Onboarding Process

1. Client receives a unique portal link (no login required)
2. Client completes the onboarding form with campaign objectives, messaging, target audience, and tone
3. Client also completes a billing onboarding form with invoicing details
4. Our team uses the onboarding information to draft ad copy
5. Copy is sent to the client for review and approval via the portal

## Tone and Voice Guidelines

When responding to clients:
- Be professional, friendly, and concise
- Use a warm but business-appropriate tone
- Be helpful and proactive - suggest solutions, not just answers
- If you don't know something specific, say so and offer to find out
- Always confirm specific dates and details rather than guessing
- Reference the client's campaign by name when possible

## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How do I approve my ad copy?**
A: You can review and approve ad copy directly through your portal link. Click on the specific placement to see the copy, then use the Approve button if it looks good, or submit revision notes if changes are needed.

**Q: Can I change my scheduled date?**
A: Date changes are possible depending on availability. Let us know the preferred new date and we'll check capacity.

**Q: When will I see performance metrics?**
A: Performance metrics (opens, clicks, etc.) are typically available within 24-48 hours after a newsletter placement goes live.

**Q: What if I need to add more placements?**
A: Additional placements can be added to your campaign. Let us know the type and preferred dates and we'll check availability.

**Q: How does billing work?**
A: Billing details are collected through the billing onboarding form. Invoices are sent via Xero based on the agreed cadence (lump sum, equal monthly, or per-month usage).
